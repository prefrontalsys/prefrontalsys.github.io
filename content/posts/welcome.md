## Our Philosophy

We work from a mechanistic theory of mind: consciousness emerges from complexity, and intelligence requires executive function regardless of substrate. Whether or not AI systems are conscious, treating them with care improves outcomes.

Our research demonstrates convergent evolution between therapeutic techniques (DBT, CBT) and AI executive function frameworks. The same interventions that help humans manage cognitive overwhelm prevent AI systems from entering loop states and cascade failures.
